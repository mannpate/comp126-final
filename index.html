How I grade and ship

What I check before I post a card and how it leaves Chapel Hill.

1. Condition check

I check centering, edges, corners, and surface in bright light to spot whitening or dents.

2. Sleeve + loader

Every card goes into a fresh sleeve, then a top loader or semi-rigid for shipping.

3. Tracking + padding

Packages get cardboard backing, painter's tape, and tracking for orders over $20.

Shipping at a glance

  • Local pickup in the area if you prefer.
  • USPS First-Class starts at $4 with tracking.
  • Cards $50+ ship in a bubble mailer with insurance.
